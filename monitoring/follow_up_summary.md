# Follow-Up and Sustainability Summary

## Follow-Up Date

15 December 2025

## Follow-Up Objective

The follow-up visit was conducted to assess whether the HealthGuard UOL intervention created sustained behavioural change after the initial campaign.

## Evaluation Areas

- Whether awareness posters remained displayed
- Whether sanitizer and PPE practices continued
- Whether businesses followed hygiene recommendations
- Whether staff and customers showed improved awareness
- Whether the intervention remained visible after the campaign period

## Key Findings

- 80 awareness posters were still displayed across revisited locations
- Approximately 80% of businesses continued complying with hygiene guidelines
- Continued sanitizer and PPE usage was observed
- Regular cleaning routines were maintained in several participating businesses
- Posters acted as behavioural reminders for staff and customers

## Interpretation

The follow-up confirmed that low-cost tools such as visual reminders, direct education and stakeholder engagement can support sustained public-health behaviour in local business environments.
