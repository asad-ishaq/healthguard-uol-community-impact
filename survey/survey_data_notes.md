# Survey Data Notes

## Purpose

This folder contains the survey instrument and survey dataset used for the HealthGuard UOL community health initiative.

The survey was designed to understand awareness, behaviour and concerns regarding communicable diseases in high-contact community spaces such as gyms, barber shops/salons, cafes/restaurants and other local businesses.

## Files

| File | Description |
|---|---|
| survey_instrument.pdf | Customer and owner survey forms used for data collection |
| survey_data.xlsx | Survey dataset containing customer and business-owner responses |

## Dataset Structure

The Excel file contains the following sheets:

| Sheet | Description |
|---|---|
| Customer_Surveys | Customer-level responses about disease awareness, visible prevention measures and perceived safety |
| Owner_Surveys | Business-owner responses about prevention measures, challenges and willingness to receive guidance |
| Measures count | Summary of visible preventive measures reported by customers |
| Safety count | Summary of customer safety perception responses |
| % of awareness | Awareness percentage summary for customers and business owners |

## Sample Size

- Customer survey responses: 51
- Owner/business survey responses: 23

## Key Variables

### Customer Survey

- Age
- Gender
- Place type
- Awareness of communicable diseases
- Known diseases
- Measures noticed
- Safety feeling
- Suggestions

### Owner Survey

- Business name
- Facility type
- Location
- Number of staff
- Awareness of communicable diseases
- Measures implemented
- Challenges faced
- Willingness to receive guidance
- Suggestions

## Data Quality Note

Some categorical responses may require cleaning before dashboarding or statistical analysis. For example, spelling/capitalization differences and multi-select responses should be standardized before final analysis.
