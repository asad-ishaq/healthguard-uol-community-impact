# SDG Alignment

## SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

HealthGuard UOL directly supports SDG 3 by promoting communicable disease prevention, hygiene awareness, sanitizer usage, PPE adoption and safer behaviour in high-contact community spaces.

## SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

The initiative contributes to safer local community spaces by targeting gyms, cafes, restaurants, retail stores and barber salons where disease transmission risks can be higher due to frequent public interaction.

## SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

The project involved collaboration between students, local business owners, community members and university stakeholders. This partnership-based approach made the intervention practical, scalable and locally relevant.

## Impact Framing

HealthGuard UOL demonstrates that student-led, low-cost, community-based interventions can create measurable and sustainable improvements in public-health behaviour.
