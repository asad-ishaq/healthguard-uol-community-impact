# Instagram Campaign Summary

## Campaign Handle

@healthguard_uol

## Campaign Purpose

The Instagram campaign was created to extend the impact of the HealthGuard UOL project beyond physical business visits. It shared public-health awareness content related to communicable disease prevention, hygiene practices and community safety.

## Content Themes

- Communicable disease awareness
- Hygiene and sanitization reminders
- PPE usage
- Disease-prevention tips
- On-ground activity documentation
- Community engagement highlights

## Hashtags Used

- #HealthGuardUOL
- #PreventDisease
- #CommunityHealth
- #StaySafePakistan
- #SDG3

## Reported Campaign Performance

- 10,000+ digital/social reach reported in project documentation
- 250+ engagements reported in project documentation
- Awareness content shared through Instagram posts, videos and stories

## Strategic Role

The social media campaign amplified on-ground interventions and helped sustain community engagement after the physical awareness activities.
